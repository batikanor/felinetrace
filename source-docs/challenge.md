Cortea - 🕵️ Follow the money. Find the fraud. Prove it.

AI can read a company dossier and shout "fraud" in seconds. But in an audit, a verdict nobody can verify is worthless. Auditors only act on findings they can trace back to real evidence.

**Your goal:**

We give you ~20 documents (German and English) from a fictional payments company. Hidden inside is a layered fraud: some obvious inconsistencies, plus deeper manipulations you only find by combining documents. Build an agent with an interactive interface an auditor can use to uncover it, where every claim links to the exact document, page, and passage it rests on. No number without a source.

You build and test on a sample dossier. Judging happens on a separate final dossier released before the deadline. What counts is whether you find the fraud there.

Watch out: some discrepancies are innocent. Flagging clean items counts against you.

Some ideas to get you started: automatic reconciliation of bank confirmations against the ledger, three-way matching of invoices, orders, and payments, or tracing any figure in the financial statements back to its source documents.

Bring whatever stack you're comfortable with. No required tech, no starter kit.
